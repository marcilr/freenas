Motherboard


make:      Supermicro
processor: Xeon
speed:     3.0Ghz or higher


Supermicro's X10 motherboards are very popular in the FreeNAS community, and one offering,
the X10SL7[1], includes an integrated LSI SAS controller, boosting storage connectivity
from 6 to a whopping 14 ports! But this is a Micro-ATX board, meaning it needs a larger
enclosure.
NOTE: This board only supports up to 32GB ram.
--blog.fosketts.net/
